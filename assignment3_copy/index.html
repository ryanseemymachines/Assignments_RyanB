Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • 1. What types of events do you manage?
  • Ans: We handle a wide range of events including corporate events, weddings, birthday parties, conferences, product launches, and social gatherings.
  • 2. Do you offer customized event planning?
  • Ans: Yes! We tailor every event according to your preferences, budget, and theme to make it unique and memorable.
  • 3. How far in advance should I book your services?
  • Ans: We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for small events and 3-6 months for larger events to ensure availability and proper planning.
  • 4. Can you manage the event budget?
  • Ans: Absolutely. We provide detailed cost estimates, suggest cost-effective solutions, and help manage your budget without compromising on quality.
  • 5. Do you provide on-site event coordination?
  • Ans: Yes, our team will be present on the day of the event to handle logistics, coordination, and ensure everything runs smoothly.
