Web development is the process of building and maintaining websites. It involves coding, design, and performance optimization to ensure a great user experience.

Front-end development focuses on the visual and interactive parts of a website. Technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript bring the design to life.

Back-end development handles the server-side logic, databases, and APIs that power a website. It ensures that everything runs smoothly behind the scenes.

Together, front-end and back-end development form the foundation of modern web applications, enabling seamless digital experiences.
